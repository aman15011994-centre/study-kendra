How to Calculate Percentage of Marks

Calculating percentage from marks is simple. Use this formula:

Formula: Percentage = (Marks Obtained ÷ Total Marks) × 100

Example 1: Single Subject

If you scored 425 marks out of 500, the calculation is:
(425 ÷ 500) × 100 = 85%

Example 2: Multiple Subjects with Same Total Marks

Suppose you scored 85, 78, 92, 88, and 76 out of 100 in five subjects. Your total obtained marks are 419 and total maximum marks are 500.
(419 ÷ 500) × 100 = 83.8%

Example 3: Subjects with Different Total Marks

Math: 80/100, Science: 70/80, English: 45/50.
Add all obtained marks and all total marks first, then apply the formula:
(80 + 70 + 45) ÷ (100 + 80 + 50) × 100 = 195 ÷ 230 × 100 = 84.78%

Note: when subjects have different maximum marks, do not average the individual percentages — always add the raw marks first, then apply the formula once for an accurate result.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate percentage from marks?

Divide the marks obtained by the total marks, then multiply by 100. For example, if you scored 425 out of 500, the calculation is (425 ÷ 500) × 100 = 85%.

Can I calculate percentage for subjects with different total marks?

Yes. Add up the obtained marks from all subjects, add up the total marks from all subjects, then divide the total obtained marks by the total maximum marks and multiply by 100.

Does this calculator work for any exam or board?
