How to Convert CGPA to Percentage

Different boards and universities use different formulas to convert CGPA into percentage. Here are the two most common ones used in India:

Formula 1 — CBSE Students (Class 10 & 12)

Formula: Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

Example: If your CBSE CGPA is 9.2, then your percentage is 9.2 × 9.5 = 87.4%

Formula 2 — Most Universities (Engineering, Degree)

Formula: Percentage = CGPA × 10

Example: If your university CGPA is 8.5, then your percentage is 8.5 × 10 = 85%

How is CGPA Calculated from Marks?

Formula: CGPA = Sum of Grade Points of All Subjects ÷ Total Number of Subjects

Example: If your grade points in 5 subjects are 9, 8, 7, 9, 8 — then CGPA = (9+8+7+9+8) ÷ 5 = 41 ÷ 5 = 8.2 CGPA

Frequently Asked Questions

How to convert CGPA to percentage?

For CBSE students: multiply CGPA by 9.5. For most universities: multiply CGPA by 10. Example — 8.0 CGPA in CBSE = 8.0 × 9.5 = 76%.

What is the CGPA to percentage formula for CBSE?

CBSE official formula is: Percentage = CGPA × 9.5. So if your CGPA is 9.2, your percentage is 9.2 × 9.5 = 87.4%.

How is CGPA calculated from marks?

Add the grade points of all subjects and divide by total number of subjects. For example, grade points 9, 8, 7, 9, 8 in 5 subjects gives CGPA = 41 ÷ 5 = 8.2.

Is 8.5 CGPA good?

Yes, 8.5 CGPA is considered very good. It equals 80.75% (CBSE) or 85% (university formula). Most companies and colleges consider 7.5+ CGPA as good for placements and admissions.
