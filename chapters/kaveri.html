About the Kaveri Book

English Kaveri is the Class 9 NCERT English textbook. It contains inspiring stories and poems that improve reading, vocabulary, grammar and writing skills.

Every lesson on Study Kendra includes carefully prepared summaries, Hindi word meanings, important questions, MCQs, revision notes and exam-oriented answers to help students score better in school and board examinations.
